Speaking Part 1 training: expanding your answers
3
With your partner, take turns to ask each other general questions about the main topic, using the notes as a prompt. For example:

Can you tell me about the
climate in your country?

Then ask specific questions to draw out the information in the notes.

Does the weather
affect what people
wear in your country?

How often do you
watch or listen to the
weather forecast?

Do you prefer a hot
or cold climate?

Try to use some of the linking words and phrases.
